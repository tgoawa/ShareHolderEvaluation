Leadership Goals

Many shareholders spend time leading an industry team or service line, working on a committee, lending their expertise to a firm-wide project or giving their time on other initiatives. These leadership behaviors make a significant contribution to the overall growth of the firm and set an example for future leaders. We will now be formally recognizing those who have leadership commitments to help grow the firm and leave a legacy; while goals within the Leadership category are optional, Competency, WIGs and Economic goals are required.

To add a goal, click on the green “Add Goal” in the Goals box below. To the right of the Goals box, is where you will add “Goal Details.” Use the drop down arrow to choose the Industry Team and/or a Service Line in which this goal applies (if applicable), give it a name and choose the weight you want to assign to this goal. Next, add a description of your goal. If applicable, describe the goal using the SMART methodology– Specific Measurable Achievable Realistic and Timely; or, the X to Y by when. See the guidebook for details on creating goals. Add specific actions that you need to take in order for you to achieve your goal.
